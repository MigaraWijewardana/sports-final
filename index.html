WHAT IS SPORTS LCM?

Sports LCM is a dynamic event by AIESEC in USJ designed to bring members together through teamwork and healthy competition. The initiative focuses on

  •   Building Connections
  •   Developing Leaders
  •   Enhancing Well-being

Our Vision

To create an energetic, inclusive environment through sports. Breaking barriers and igniting passion and unity.

WHAT TO EXPECT

  • High-stakes futsal tournament for the front offices of AIESEC in USJ.

  • Interactive mini-games and strategy challenges designed to test intellect alongside physical prowess.

  • A dedicated zone for networking, and cross-functional bonding.
